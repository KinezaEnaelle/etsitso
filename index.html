Factors behind a marketing campaign sucess

A successful marketing campaign depends on a number of factors, both on the side of the agency and on the side of the client company. Whether you are on one side or the other, from the moment you execute a campaign the responsibility for success falls on both of you.
Some of the factors affecting campaigns may be beyond your control but, without doubt, others are totally manageable. Based on our experience, we've put together 5 key elements among others that you should consider for every marketing campaign, to maximize your chances of success.
1.Clearly stating the scope of the work and expectations from both parties: It is possible that due to various factors a marketing campaign essentials steps to launch such as the briefing are rushed. That’s a bad start. A recipe for an ineffective campaign. It is fundamental to resist the pressure to launch and do things the right way. Blurry expectations are an open ground to disagreements between the two parties.
2.Effective communication and collaboration: One cannot highlight enough the importance of effective communication and collaboration. For a better collaboration, it is essential to have an understanding of individual roles. Each part should know clearly from the on-set expected deliverable tasks and mid/long term goals. This enables highlighting critical tasks to not miss and avoiding ineffectiveness. For the communication side, constant in-person and online dialogue is very important. Multiple communication options and channels must be put in place. This is fundamental to encourage feedback and foster an environment of trust. Fluid communication ultimately boosts progress in tasks execution. Thirdly, conflict will surely arise. Even when you execute planned tasks effectively, issues and misunderstandings will arise. Better prepare for such situation by laying down conflict resolutions strategies. Win-Win scenarios are key to resolve these situations.
3.Thorough mapping of the actions end-result allowing flexibility in execution: When executing ground activities, there are always unexpected situations where teams have to adjust. Having already defined campaigns goals allows to adapt to field execution challenges without compromising the end goal. Both parties need to be flexible to adjust to new realities keeping in mind expected actions outcomes.
4.Creativity conducive work environment: It is crucial to set up a work environment where creative agencies feel free to express their creativity and enrich the campaigns sometimes on-the-go. Project champions need to keep an open door attitude toward new concepts provide they fit in schedule and serve project ultimate purpose.
5.Openness to Out-of-Box creative concepts: One of the character trait of creative marketing teams is their bubbling creative mind. You’re guaranteed to never get bored. While executing campaigns, it is much expected that new creative concepts emerge. Probing such ideas and encouraging them make your campaign go a long way.
